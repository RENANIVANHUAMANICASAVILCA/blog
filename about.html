Researcher, IB Student & Software Developer

Profile & Description

I am a researcher at PlanetLab, Intinauta Research Center, and an Informatics Engineering student at Pontificia Universidad Catolica del Peru. My work connects software development, remote sensing, astrodynamics, and scientific problem-solving.

I am interested in neuroscience, biotechnology, medicine, environmental science, and the systems that turn complex data into useful tools. I enjoy building software that bridges research, visual analysis, and real-world impact.
